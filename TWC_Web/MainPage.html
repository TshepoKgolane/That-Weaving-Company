about us

what makes our materials special?

The TWC Company manufactures belts made from polypropylene thread weaved together in a 2200 denier and 1000 denier threads that are capable of lifting half a ton to one-and-a-half ton while being extremely reliable

The TWC Company only uses the highest quality polypropylene sourced from suppliers it also uses the best machinery in threading the polypropylene to become the best weaved belts on the market
